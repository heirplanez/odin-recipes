(Baked) Fried Wings


Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (such as Kraft®)
  • 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 pounds chicken wings, tips discarded
  • ½ cup melted butter
