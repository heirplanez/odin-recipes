Slow Cooker Sesame Chicken

Description

This tasty meal can be made on a busy day which is perfect for those on the run. Drop your ingredients in the crockpot, set the temperature and go about your day knowing you'll have a delicious treat waiting for you after a long day.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • sesame seeds to taste

Steps

  1. Spread chicken into the crock of a slow cooker.
  2. Cook on Low until the chicken is tender, 3 to 4 hours.
  3. Remove chicken from the sauce in the slow cooker to a cutting board. Stir water and cornstarch together to dissolve cornstarch completely; pour into the sauce in the slow cooker until smooth.
  4. Cook on High until slightly thickened, about 10 minutes.
  5. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces and stir into the sauce. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the chicken and sauce mixture.
