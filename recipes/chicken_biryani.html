Chicken Biryani

Description

Essentially, it’s your favourite chicken curry (or vegetable or other protein of choice) buried under a mound of delicately spiced fluffy rice, all made in one pot. The rice is steamed over a low heat so it absorbs the flavours of the curry bubbling away underneath.

So in a nutshell, it’s every curry loving-carb monsters’ dream come true. It’s got my name written all over it!

Ingredients

    Biryani

  • 750g (1.5 lb) chicken thighs , skin on, bone in, halved along bone (Note 1)
  • 1 cup coriander / cilantro , chopped
  • 1/4 cup (60g) ghee or unsalted butter , melted

    • Saffron

    • 1 tsp saffron threads (loosely packed)
    • 2 tbsp warm water

Steps

    Saffron

    • Place in a bowl, leave for 10 minutes+.

    Biryani

  1. Place pot with chicken in it onto a stove over medium heat. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove lid. Cook for 5 minutes, turning chicken twice.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Turn chicken so skin side is down - it should cover most of the base of the pot.
  5. Drizzle saffron over ghee.
  6. Place lid on. Return to stove over medium heat.
  7. As soon as you see steam, turn down to low then cook for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove from stove, rest with lid on for 10 minutes.
